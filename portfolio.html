Tanuki Samurai Showdown

When I first got started in game development, I found an incredible tutorial online on making 2D games.
To get my feet wet, I decided to hop into the tutorial and loved every second of it. Seeing every line I wrote
come to life was a rush I had never felt before. Once I had finished the tutorial, I decided to take it a step further and
added sound effects and music, options for audio settings, a start menu, etc. I truly felt like I was in my craft.
I've since moved on from this project and have worked on a few other small projects, but nothing as finished as this one.
